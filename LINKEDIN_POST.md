🛣️ Just shipped **RoadSense AI** for #vibe2ship — Coding Ninjas × Google for Developers!

India loses ₹50,000 Cr a year to potholes, and road complaints have the worst resolution rate of
any civic category. So I built the missing operational layer between citizens and repair crews.

Snap a pothole → a 6-agent Gemini pipeline classifies it, scores its real-world impact, generates a
work order, dispatches a crew, and an AI audit proves the repair with before/after photos.

The part I loved building: a **Civic Copilot** — a real Gemini *function-calling* agent that can list
& prioritise issues, file reports, dispatch crews and ground answers with Google Search + Maps, live.

What I used from the Google stack:
⚡ Gemini 2.5 (Vision + Function Calling)
🌐 Grounding with Google Search + Maps + URL context
☁️ Deployed on Google Cloud Run (built in Google AI Studio)
📱 Shipped as an installable mobile PWA

Biggest takeaway: tool-calling agents turn a "demo" into something that actually *does the work*.
